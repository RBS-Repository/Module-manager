Guest Information We Collect

Makati Island Resort collects and receives certain information from our guests and website visitors, including the activities you book on our island, your device information, and data obtained from cookies. For example, when you visit our website or make a reservation, we automatically collect your IP address, browser type, and access times to provide you with the best experience.

When you make a reservation at Makati Island Resort, we collect some Personal Information about you such as:

  • Your country of residence and travel details
  • Your full name, email address and contact information
  • Your unique guest ID which is assigned upon reservation
  • Special requests and preferences for your stay
  • Your passport information for check-in purposes
  • Your payment information when booking activities or making purchases at our resort
  • Photos you share on social media with our resort hashtags
  • Feedback and reviews about your stay
  • Communications regarding your reservation or stay

How We Use Your Information

Makati Island Resort uses guest data for the following purposes:

  1. To process your reservation and check-in
  2. To provide our resort services and activities
  3. To process payments and confirm bookings
  4. To analyze and improve our services and guest experience
  5. To communicate with you about your stay
  6. To share anonymous statistics about resort operations
  7. To send you updates about special offers and events
  8. To market Makati Island Resort services
  9. To better understand guest preferences and improve amenities
  10. To maintain resort security and safety
  11. To comply with legal requirements and industry standards

Our Resort Services

Makati Island is a luxury island resort offering world-class accommodations and experiences. Our services include beachfront villas, water sports, spa treatments, dining venues, and customized island tours.

We strive to create unforgettable memories while protecting your privacy and ensuring your comfort throughout your stay.

Your Consent

By making a reservation or using any of our services at Makati Island Resort, you consent to the collection, transfer, storage, and use of your Personal Information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our privacy practices, please do not use our services.

Request Your Information

Makati Island Resort uses secure tracking technology to enhance your resort experience and provide personalized service. We respect your right to access your personal data.

You may request information about what data we have collected during your stays with us. We are committed to transparency and protecting your Personal Information
